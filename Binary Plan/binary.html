Binary Plan Overview

The Binary Plan is very well known and easiest MLM Plan around MLM world who wants to make easy money from their location or work places.

In MLM (multi-level marketing) network or world where new comers join into Binary Tree structure i.e. one on left member and another on right member in the sub-tree.

Usually, one side sub-tree is referred to as Power leg and the other side is called as Profit leg.

Power leg grows with new member joining, even introduced by previously enrolled member.

New members in the power leg placed under a leaf available position of the binary tree, when a member works to grow his binary tree structure under Profit leg, some commission is calculated

and disturbed by a formula using certain value matched with Power leg that may be 2:1 or 1:2 next pair onwards
